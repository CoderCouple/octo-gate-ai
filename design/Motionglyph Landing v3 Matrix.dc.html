Why this works

Every dot is identical in size, color, and density. The word exists only as coherent motion — a signal human vision groups instantly ("common fate"), and one that image-based models analyzing frames never see.

> Humans pass fast

No distorted letters, no fire hydrants. The moving word pops out of the noise in under a second, at any age and reading level.

> Screenshots are empty

Pause the challenge or download a frame: a frozen image is statistically flat noise. There is no glyph to OCR, so vision-model attacks return nothing.

> Decoys punish analysis

A static decoy word hides in every challenge. Agents doing frame analysis find it, answer confidently — and answer wrong. Motion vectors randomize per challenge.
